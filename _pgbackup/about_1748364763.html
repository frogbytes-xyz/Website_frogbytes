About us

Bridging the gap between complex AI agents and effective testing

Our story began in late 2020 as Flowrite, with a vision to redefine email productivity with AI. Over the next few years, we built Flowrite into the leading AI email assistant for Gmail, serving hundreds of thousands of users across 150+ countries.

In 2024, our Flowrite product was acquired Maestro Labs, enabling us to double down on building cutting-edge AI tooling.

At Flow AI, we’ve taken our expertise in AI-driven communication to a broader challenge—helping AI teams build and refine agents with confidence.

Today, we collaborate with leading AI companies to push the boundaries of language models, working at the intersection of research and applied AI to shape the future of agentic AI systems.

Our solution

Powering AI agent testing at scale

AI teams move fast. Testing shouldn’t slow them down. Flow AI makes it effortless to create and refine high-quality testing datasets—ensuring AI agents perform reliably in the real world.
